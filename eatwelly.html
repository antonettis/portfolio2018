EatWelly
App

Client: EatWelly
School project

EatWelly is a local app dedicated to budget friendly restaurants in Wellington City. It is primarily aimed at students and tourists who want to enjoy delicious, nutritious foods in a welcoming atmosphere without breaking the bank. The app was developed in partnership with the Wellington City Council who wishes to promote healthy eating and reduce frequentation of fast food places. We also partnered with local restaurant who provided us with their menus and recipes. This allowed us to determine the nutritional content of their popular dishes.
This project was made in collaboration with my classmate Nikolai Bain.
